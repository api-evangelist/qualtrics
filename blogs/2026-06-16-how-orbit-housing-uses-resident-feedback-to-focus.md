Orbit Housing, managing over 46,000 UK homes, partnered with Qualtrics to gather resident feedback across multiple channels. The platform analyzes customer input to identify emerging trends and automatically flag concerns, enabling the organization to respond quickly and improve services as part of its Everyday Excellence transformation program aimed at delivering high-quality, customer-focused housing services.
