Research from over 7,000 consumers reveals that understanding is most crucial for resolving customer issues, yet AI agents score lowest on this dimension. While AI scores highest on friendliness (4.41), it lags significantly on understanding (4.08), creating a gap that frustrates customers and drives churn.
